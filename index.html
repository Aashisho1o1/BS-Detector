      How It Works

      Every claim passes through a multi-step conflict-of-interest causal chain.

      📢
      Claim Input
      Public statement from a tech leader, with speaker identified
      📋
      Decompose
      Extract implied action, economic domain, and extremity score
      🔭
      Hypothesize
      Generate 5 independent causal chains: who economically benefits?
      🔗
      Match Ties
      Cross-reference beneficiaries against the speaker's financial ties DB
      📊
      BS Score
      Weighted 0–100 percentile: connection rate, directness, magnitude, extremity
      Score Interpretation
      0–20
      Mostly Genuine
      Claim has limited direct financial benefit for speaker
      20–40
      Mildly Biased
      Some indirect ties exist, but claim may still be valid
      40–65
      Somewhat Biased
      Clear conflicts present — read carefully
      65–85
      Self-Serving
      Multiple direct financial ties confirmed
      85–100
      Highly Self-Serving
      Textbook "talking their book" — extreme caution advised
